Atomic Habits

The Identity Shift

Don't focus on outcomes; focus on identity. Every action is a vote for the person you want to become.

Action: Define your "Type" (e.g., "I am an author") and perform one small action today that validates that identity.

Influence

The Rule of Reciprocity

Humans are hard-wired to repay favors. If you provide genuine value before asking for anything, you create natural momentum in any relationship.

Action: Identify one person you need cooperation from and provide them with a small, unsolicited value add this week.

Deep Work

Managing Attention Residue

Your brain doesn't switch tasks instantly. "Attention residue" from unfinished tasks kills potential. You must finish one complex task entirely before starting the next.

Action: Use a "Shutdown Ritual" at the end of the day to close all open loops so your brain doesn't carry residue into tomorrow.

Thinking, Fast and Slow

System 2 Verification

System 1 (your gut) is lazy and prone to shortcuts. When stakes are high, force System 2 (logic) to pause and verify the raw data before making a move.

Action: On your next high-stakes decision, write down the three biggest assumptions you are making and explicitly fact-check them.

The Psychology of Money

Margin of Safety

The most important part of any plan is planning on the plan not going according to plan. Build a "room for error" to ensure you don't quit during inevitable volatility.

Action: Look at your current project. Ask: "If this goes 30% off-track, what is the one thing I must hold steady to stay in the game?"
